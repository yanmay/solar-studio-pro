1. Product Overview
1.1  What Is SolarScan AI?
SolarScan AI is a browser-based SaaS tool that lets homeowners, commercial property owners, and solar installers instantly evaluate rooftop solar potential for any address in India — without requiring a physical site visit or specialised engineering knowledge.
A user enters an address or pincode, traces their rooftop boundary on satellite imagery, configures panel preferences and electricity tariffs, and receives a full financial feasibility report including projected ROI, payback period, monthly energy harvest, government subsidy eligibility, and carbon savings — all within minutes.
1.2  The Problem
The Indian rooftop solar market is growing at 30%+ YoY, yet the sales funnel is broken at the top:
Homeowners have no reliable way to self-assess if solar is viable for their specific roof before inviting a vendor.
Solar installers spend 2–4 hours per lead on manual site surveys, burning cost on unqualified prospects.
Misinformation on pricing and subsidies (especially PM Surya Ghar) creates distrust and stalls decisions.
The pre-purchase research phase is dominated by generic content — nothing personalised to an individual rooftop.
Core insight
The gap is not awareness — it is conviction. Homeowners want quantified proof before committing. SolarScan AI turns a satellite map into a personalised business case.
1.3  The Solution
SolarScan AI bridges the research-to-intent gap by giving any user a satellite-based, AI-assisted rooftop analysis in under 5 minutes. The platform combines:
Satellite imagery + manual rooftop tracing for precise roof area calculation.
Configurable panel layout engine with real-time panel count, shading, and setback modelling.
Financial modelling engine (tariff-aware ROI, EMI, subsidy) calibrated for India.
A paywall-gated PDF report that converts interest into a paid transaction.

5. MVP Definition
MVP principle
The MVP is the smallest working product that lets a paying user go from address → traced roof → financial report → purchased PDF download in one unbroken session.
5.1  In Scope for MVP
Functional landing page with address search and sandbox metros.
Globe → Satellite map transition with functional rooftop tracing (polygon + area calc).
3-step layout configurator producing a valid panel count and kWp system size.
Results page with all financial metrics computed correctly (tariff-aware, subsidy-capped).
Recharts: 25-year cashflow chart + monthly harvest bar chart.
Carbon offset card.
Paywall overlay with simulated payment UI sufficient for demo; Razorpay integration required before real user payments.
PDF export (jsPDF) covering all core report sections.
Mobile-readable layout for results page (full mobile editor is v1.1).
5.2  MVP Cuts (Explicitly Out of Scope)
User accounts / authentication — no login; session-based only in MVP.
Saved scans history — no database persistence for user data.
Real payment gateway — simulated checkout suffices for investor demo; required before public launch.
Multi-roof tracing or L-shaped polygon merge.
Shade obstacle drawing.
Shareable results URL.
Mobile-optimised map editor.
AI panel-layout optimisation.
Live DISCOM tariff API.
Installer lead-gen CRM integration.

8. Technical Constraints & Assumptions
Area
Constraint / Assumption
Satellite tile provider
Google Satellite via Leaflet; Esri as fallback. API key costs must be budgeted by monthly tile request volume.
Solar irradiance data
Currently inferred from tilt/location heuristics. NASA POWER API integration is deferred but referenced in marketing copy — align engineering on actual data source before launch.
Financial model
All calculations are estimates. Legal disclaimer required on results page: 'Actual yields may vary ±15–20% based on local shading, panel degradation, and DISCOM charges.'
Subsidy calculation
PM Surya Ghar cap of ₹78,000 based on 2024 scheme rules. Needs quarterly review as scheme parameters change.
Payment
Simulated checkout in MVP. Razorpay or Cashfree integration (UPI + cards) required before charging real users. GST compliance needed from Day 1.
Browser support
Chrome 90+, Safari 15+, Firefox 90+. IE11 not supported. Three.js globe degrades gracefully on unsupported WebGL devices.
Data privacy
No user PII stored in MVP (session-only). When accounts launch, DPDPA 2023 compliance framework required.

Appendix: Glossary
Term
Definition
kWp
Kilowatt-peak — the rated output of a solar installation under standard test conditions
DISCOM
Distribution Company — the regional electricity utility that manages grid connection and tariffs
PM Surya Ghar
Indian government rooftop solar subsidy scheme offering up to ₹78,000 for residential installations
Turf.js
Open-source geospatial analysis library used to compute polygon areas from map coordinates
Setback
Mandatory clear space between the roof edge and the nearest solar panel row, required for safety and maintenance
C&I
Commercial & Industrial — the non-residential solar segment, typically ≥ 10 kWp systems
ROI Tuning Console
Interactive panel on the results page that lets users adjust tariff, panel count, and grade to recalculate financials in real time
Pay-per-Scan
One-time payment (₹149) to unlock a single full scan report, as opposed to the monthly Pro subscription
