SolarNet AI Technology

Uses advanced geocoding and GIS boundaries mapped with NASA POWER API solar radiation data and local state utility tariff rules to output complete generation reports. SolarNet matches your GPS location with regional weather trends, tracking average peak sun hours to ensure generation models are accurate and customized for your exact coordinates.

PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana Subsidies

The Government of India provides national subsidies for residential solar installations under the PM Surya Ghar scheme. SUNPOWER LINK integrates these subsidy rules automatically, estimating up to ₹78,000 in direct financial assistance for systems up to 3 kWp. We break down the gross installation costs, government incentives, and net outlays so you can make informed decisions about your renewable energy transitions.

Electricity Tariff & Financial Payback Projections

Our financial ledger maps slab rates, fixed charges, and taxes for key state utilities in India (including MSEDCL in Maharashtra and BESCOM in Karnataka) to calculate monthly and annual savings. By contrasting generation yields against grid rates, we estimate your break-even payback period and model cumulative 25-year return on investment (ROI) metrics.

Environmental Benefits & Carbon Offsets

By switching to rooftop solar power, you reduce reliance on coal-heavy grid electricity in India. SUNPOWER LINK computes the exact carbon emissions avoided annually and scales the positive impact to an equivalent number of fully matured trees planted over a 25-year system lifespan.
